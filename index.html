Based on Leading Educational Research

SmartyPAL's expert-developed skills framework is built around a diversity of skills that 21st century children need to succeed in both formal and informal education. While children play, our platform inconspicuously measures their sklil levels and reports key insights back to parents.

Spatial analysis

Pattern recognition and mental manipulation of visual shapes and objects.

Language & Literacy

Letter and word recognition, comprehension, spelling, and reading proficiency.

Emotional & cognitive empathy

Understanding what others are thinking, feeling, and perceiving.

Creativity & problem solving

Outside-the-box thinking for solving multi-step tasks.
